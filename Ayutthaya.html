One of the best ways to experience Ayutthaya is by exploring its temple ruins, scattered throughout the historical park. Each temple tells its own story and offers a unique perspective on the ancient city’s religious and cultural life.

Tips for Visiting Ayutthaya

  • Best Time to Visit: The cooler months from November to February are ideal.
  • What to Wear: Dress respectfully, covering shoulders and knees when visiting temples.
  • Bring Water: The heat can be intense, so stay hydrated!

Welcome to M.A.D.venture

Miles And Distance | Exploring the World, One Journey at a Time


MADventure or Miles And Distance Venture is our family's journey of exploration, growth, and discovery. Our MAD blog is all about embracing every step of the way — whether across rugged landscapes, through vibrant cities, or around the world at large.


Through our travels, we aim to share not just our experiences but also the lessons learned along the journey. From the tough and challenging moments to the smooth and joyful ones, every part of the adventure is an opportunity to grow.


As parents, we want our three children to explore, grow, and learn from each place we visit, each culture we experience, and each lesson we encounter. M.A.D.venture is about embracing both the beauty and the challenges of travel, and finding strength in every mile, every discovery, and every distance we cover together.
